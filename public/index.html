Spotdataba.se

In my free time I like to skate on the streets with my friends, finding new spots to skate is something we love to do. But letting others know about them is harder.

To solve this I created a mobile friendly web application that let's you easily add spots with a photo and a description, both from your phone and desktop browsers.

In the first week of the soft launch, people from all over the Netherlands added just shy of 200 spots.

See it in action at Spotdataba.se
